# PRODUCT VISION

Amaroo aims to become the most trusted and intuitive marketplace for Bangladesh, giving buyers and merchants a bilingual, reliable, and transparent experience.

## Buyer promise

- Bangla + English first-class UI
- Clear pricing, delivery, and return visibility
- Trusted local payment options including COD
- Fast search and mobile-first browsing

## Merchant promise

- Straightforward onboarding and operations
- Real-time inventory and order tools
- Courier and payment integrations tailored for Bangladesh
- Transparent settlement and issue-resolution workflows

## Platform principles

1. Trust over gimmicks
2. Clarity over clutter
3. Local relevance at every step
4. Scalable architecture for nationwide growth
